Paul "'''ReDeYe'''" Chaloner, is one of the world's first professional esports and video game broadcast commentators. He commentates on a myriad of games, including ''[[wikipedia:Quake 3|Quake 3]]'', ''[[wikipedia:Quake 4|Quake 4]]'', ''[[counterstrike:Counter-Strike: Source|Counter-Strike: Source]]'', ''[[counterstrike:Counter-Strike: Global Offensive|Counter-Strike: Global Offensive]]'', ''[[sc2:StarCraft 2|StarCraft 2]]'' and ''[[dota2:Dota 2|Dota 2]]'', and has broadcast in dozens of countries like China, United States, Singapore, Australia and Denmark.
