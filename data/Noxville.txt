Ben '''"Noxville"''' Steenhuisen is a South African Dota 2 statsman, doing in-game statistics, production statistics and talent preparation for various events including [[The International 2015]], [[Frankfurt_Major/2015|The Frankfurt Major]] and [[Manila_Major/2016|The Manila Major]]. He works as a software developer at an eSports analytics company.
